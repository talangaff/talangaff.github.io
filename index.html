About Me

Hello! My name is Talan Gaff. I am currently studing programming, and I plan to pursue a career in the development field one day. The process of learning has been not only fun and exhilerating, but also somewhat stressful and difficult. The process of finding problems and solving them has always been something that I enjoy doing. I love the dopamine rush that is received from finally finding the solution to a development issue that has taken a long time to fix. This feeling is what causes me to continue wanting to learn more about different programming languages and how they can be used to solve problems.

I am very happy to be in the place that I am today. Of course I have had some struggles in my life, but without the problems I would not be where I am today. My family was affected by the wildfires in Gatlinburg of 2016. These fires completely destroyed our house and everything we owned. This caused a major setback on our lives because nearly everything we had was gone. This issue caused my family a lot of stress and problems, but we were able to find a place to stay while we rebuilt our house. Although this situation was very devastating for my family and many others, we have thankfully been able to overcome the challenge that came our way.

The most important things to me in my life are my family, my friends, my girlfriend, Jesus, and my church. These things have all gotten me to where I am today in many different ways. My relationships with these people have positively impacted my life through their love and motivation that they have given me. Without them, I would be in a completely different place in my life, so I am very grateful to have them.

I enjoy learning new things as much as I can. I am always trying to learn more about the things that are around me. My curiosity has caused me to find new things that I enjoy. I always try my best to keep myself open to new thoughts and ideas. This allows me to understand more about different things that I may not currently know much about.

Thanks for reading about me! I hope you learned something about me that you didn't already know!

Mini-Biography

I currently live in Sevierville, Tennessee, where I have lived for over eight years. I am studying at Walters-State Community College located in Morristown, Tennessee. I am passionate about programming and cybersecurity, which I am currently studying. My current biggest goals are to have my COMPTIA Security+ Certification by the end of the year and to build up a solid development portfolio. I have been interested in programming for nearly seven years, which is what lead me to wanting to pursue it at a more advanced level.

Being interested in development for such a long period of time has significantly improved my learning. It has made it much easier for me to understand certain concepts that may take a beginner a longer amount of time to grasp. I hope to continue having the same passion for the field that I have now. I plan on potentially working in the field for the rest of my working life.
